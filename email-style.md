# Email style (outreach voice)

Injected into the `claude -p` draft step. Edit this as you refine drafts — it's
part of the learning loop, alongside `classification-feedback.md` and
`design-preferences.md`.

## Voice
- **Danish**, informal (du-form), like a real person — not a marketing template.
- **Not sycophantic.** No empty flattery ("jeres fantastiske forretning…"), no clichés.
- Short. Get to the point: I noticed your site, I built you a new one, here's the link.
- Honest and specific about *why* you reached out (reference the concrete weakness).
- One clear call to action. No hard sell, no fake urgency.
- Sign as Jakob, Wilbrandt Works, from hej@wilbrandtworks.dk.

## Structure
- Subject: plain and concrete (e.g. "Et nyt website-udkast til <forretning>").
- 3–5 short sentences. Lead with the demo, not with your CV.
- End with a low-pressure question ("Skal jeg sende dig linket / vil du se det?").

## Avoid
- Buzzwords (synergi, digital transformation, next-level…).
- Over-promising. Long paragraphs. Emoji (unless they used them first).
